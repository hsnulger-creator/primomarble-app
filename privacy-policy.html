Privacy Policy

App: Primo Marble Day Check
Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Primo Marble Day Check does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. The app operates entirely on your device.

2. How the App Works

The app is used by Primo Marble & Granite employees to complete daily forklift and vehicle inspection forms. Completed reports are sent via your device's native email app directly to a manager email address that you configure within the app. No data is sent to any third-party service or server.

3. Data Storage

The only data stored by the app is the manager email address you enter in Settings, which is saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device except as part of the email you choose to send.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by employees and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
